New Ways
to Enjoy

These products, of course, do not belong to dietary nutrition, but with the right selection of ingredients, they can be useful. Sweets and desserts are an integral part of the adult and children's diet. The insidiousness of such products lies in the fact that a small piece contains a large amount of free carbohydrates. Eating desserts is not always bad

When planning a daily diet, the caloric content of all foods, including sweets, should be taken into account. With proper daily use of desserts, you can bring significant benefits to the body without spoiling your figure.

Dairy and cottage cheese desserts are an additional source of protein and calcium.

A small amount of sugar is necessary to stimulate mental activity.

Desserts made from fruits and berries enrich the body with vitamins and increase immunity.
